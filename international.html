Come to Toronto, Canada

Study and live in the Greater Toronto Area

Arts and sports. Night life and nature. Food and fun. Being located across the Greater Toronto Area (GTA) means Sheridan students get unlimited access to Canada’s biggest city, cultural capital and business centre.

Our campuses are located in three Ontario cities
very close to Toronto: Oakville, Brampton and
Mississauga.
